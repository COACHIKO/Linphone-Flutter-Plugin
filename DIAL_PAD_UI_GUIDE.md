### Touch Targets
- All buttons: Minimum 48x48dp
- Recommended: 60-75dp for dial buttons
- Call button: 80dp for easy tapping

- Clear pressed states
- Color contrast ratio > 4.5:1
- Large text (28sp for numbers)

### Haptic Feedback
- Light: Digit press
- Medium: Delete/Clear
- Heavy: Call action

### Screen Reader Support
- All buttons labeled
- State announcements
- Navigation hints
